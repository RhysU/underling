Benchmarking

Underling provides an MPI-based benchmarking utility named underling_bench.

underling_bench is built and installed alongside the library. It can plan and perform various transpose and transform options to simplify measuring performance and scalability on your favorite system. It may also be used to generate FFTW wisdom for specific problem sizes. When underling is configured to use GRVY (see Installation), underling_bench provides much more detailed performance information. See underling_bench –help for more details.
